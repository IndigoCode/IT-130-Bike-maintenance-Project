What Does the chain do?

bicycle chains transfer the movment of you pushing the pedals to the drive of the bike allowing the wheel to properly spin
    What to do to make your chain perform properly?
  • Most bike chains require some amount of slack so before riding check if the chain is too lose or tight
  • Re-lube the chain esicapply after use in rain as a dry chain can cause unnecessary wear on the drivetrain
  • Wiping down the chain to prevent build-up of gunk
  • Change out worn out chains, sometimes things even though treated well break down
